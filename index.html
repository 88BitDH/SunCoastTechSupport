FAQ

The common questions people ask before they book.

What’s included in “Device Cleanup & Migration”?

It’s the “make it all sane again” package: moving your data to the new device, organizing the essentials, doing updates/performance cleanup, confirming backups, and optionally securely wiping old devices. Recycling/resale guidance is included so you know what to do with the old gear.

What if my job takes longer than the package time?

We’ll tell you early. Most jobs fit within one visit. If you need more time, you can approve an additional block before we proceed. No surprise bills.

Do you offer remote support?

Yes. If it can be solved remotely, we’ll recommend that first (faster and cheaper). For Wi‑Fi coverage, printer hardware issues, or physical device moves, onsite is usually best.

Is my data safe with you?

We use a privacy-first approach: no passwords collected via forms, and we confirm data-handling steps before any transfers or wiping. If secure wiping is requested, we confirm the method and provide documentation of what was done.

Do you take old equipment away?

Yes, as an add-on to Package A (or when paired with a service visit). We focus on responsible disposal: reuse/resale/donate when possible, recycle when appropriate. We avoid hauling low-value items as a standalone visit unless scheduled with other work.

What areas do you serve?

Tampa Bay with priority in Clearwater, Dunedin, Largo, and Palm Harbor. If you’re nearby, ask—we may still be able to help.
